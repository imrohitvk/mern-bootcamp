# MERN Bootcamp  
### Full-Stack Development Course  
**Organized by VLED Lab under the guidance of Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar**

---

## Overview

The **MERN Bootcamp** is a comprehensive **skill development course** conducted by **VLED Lab** under the guidance of **Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar** at IIT Ropar.

This self-paced bootcamp is designed to help learners master full-stack development with the MERN stack. Participants complete structured learning modules on the **ViBe platform** covering TypeScript, React, Express.js, and MongoDB.

**Key Points:**
- **No stipend provided** - This is a learning-focused program
- **No mandatory projects** - Focus is on course completion
- **ViBe platform completion is the primary requirement**
- **Case studies are optional** for extra knowledge
- Top performers may receive recommendations or internship opportunities

---

## Program Objectives

- Provide structured, self-paced learning through the ViBe platform
- Build strong foundational and advanced skills in MERN stack development
- Enable participants to master TypeScript, React, Express.js, and MongoDB
- Offer optional case studies for deeper learning and practice
- Identify and support top performers with recommendations and internship opportunities  

---

## Bootcamp Structure

The bootcamp is a **self-paced learning course** with the following structure:

---

### Core Learning: ViBe Platform (Required)

**Purpose:** Master full-stack development with the MERN stack

**What's Included:**
- Structured video lessons covering all MERN technologies
- Interactive quizzes and assessments
- Automated progress tracking
- Prerequisite modules for foundational readiness
- Advanced modules for full-stack development

**Technologies Covered:**
- TypeScript - Type-safe JavaScript fundamentals
- React - Modern frontend development
- Express.js - Backend API development
- MongoDB - NoSQL database operations

**Completion Requirement:** Finish all ViBe platform modules

---

### Supplementary Learning: Case Studies (Optional)

**Purpose:** Extra knowledge and hands-on practice

**What's Included:**
- Documentation-based lessons organized by technology
- Real-world problem-solving exercises
- Code examples and best practices
- Practical implementation tasks

**Note:** Case studies are **not required** for bootcamp completion. They are provided as optional resources for participants who want deeper understanding and additional practice.

---

### Recognition and Opportunities (Performance-Based)

**For Participants Who Excel:**
- Recommendations from Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar and VLED Lab teaching team
- Internship opportunities with VLED Lab
- Potential involvement in real-world projects

**Optional Peer Endorsements:**
- Participants who complete case studies may participate in peer evaluations
- Endorsements validate additional learning beyond core requirements  

---

## Mentorship and Support

- Continuous mentor interaction through a structured support forum  
- Dedicated guidance for doubt resolution and problem-solving  
- Team-based collaboration to strengthen communication and shared ownership  

Mentorship ensures clarity, consistency, and effective learning progression.

---

## Completion Requirements

**To complete the MERN Bootcamp, participants must:**

**Optional Activities:**
- Case study completion (for extra learning)
- Peer endorsements (for additional recognition)
